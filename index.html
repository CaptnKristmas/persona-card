Alex the Cautious Student

Price-Sensitive Undergraduate Junior

Alex is a junior in an undergraduate program who enjoys playing multiplayer PC games with friends after classes. He is cautious, analytical and patient; often waiting days or weeks for price drops before purchasing.

Goals

  • Find affordable gaming equipment that is reliable and durable.
  • Verify that reviews and product information are trustworthy.
  • Avoid wasting money on poor-quality or incompatible products.

Frustrations

  • Many online reviews tend to be untrustworthy. "Compatibility and whether reviews are fake."
  • Product specifications are inconsistent or confusing. Specs not matching across sites. "One page says it works on Mac, another says Windows only. I almost bought the wrong thing."
  • Manipulative UI elements like pop-ups and fake urgency timers. "Pop-ups, fake urgency timers, and “recommended” expensive items."

Behaviors

  • Watches Youtube reviews and reads Reddit discussions. "Amazon, Reddit, YouTube, Google Sheets to track prices."
  • Avoids impulsive purchases. "If I feel rushed, I won’t buy."
  • Tracks options manually. "Amazon, Reddit, YouTube, Google Sheets to track prices."
